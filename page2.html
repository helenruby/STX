Day 1:
Welcome to Siem Reap

Arrival in Siem Reap. Welcome by personal driver, transfer to your hotel and free time for the rest of the day. Night at the hotel.


Day 2:
Bike trip to Phnom Kulen

This morning let’s try our mountain bikes and set up our helmets to start our bike adventures. We will reach the Bantey Srei temple, the citadel of women. The challenge will start with the ascent of Phnim Kulen (+350m high). Swim brake at the waterfall, discovery of the sculpted Lingas. Then we will continue our bike trip to the peak. Hosted by local families for the night (40km far by bike).


Day 3:
Countryside and rural life by bike

In the early hours, we will ride our bikes again for a trip to the isolated villages of the Phnom Kulen mountain. We will meet the local artisans, farmers and students… We will discover the Cambodian way of life and the development projects. On our way back to our hosts, we will prepare our meal together. Promenade in the village. Hosted by local families for the night.


Day 4:
Srah Dumrei by bike

We will ride our bikes again after breakfast to reach the waterfall, passing through the jungle, limestone plains, and long grass. During our trip, we will discover the exceptional site of Srah Dumrei, a huge 6m high sandstone monolith built in the 10th century, representing elephant and lions lost in the jungle. On our way back, we will discover the lost temple of Beng Melea, still trapped in the wild jungle. Night at the hotel (30km far by bike)


Day 5:
Discovery of Angkor by bike

Today, we will go to the famous archeologic park of Angkor. We will discover the main temples of Angkor Vat which we will reach passing by small paths through the forest. Then we will continue towards the south gate of Angkor Thom to discover the walls of the ancient city buried in the forest. Then we will visit the temple of the 4 faces towers: the Bayon. Plan for this day: Angkor Thom, Ta Prom. Ta Nei, Angkor Vat. Finally, in the afternoon, an aromatherapy massage will be offered at the Frangipani Spa. Night at the hotel (30km far by bike)
